chickity china the chinese chicken

ingredients

  • 600g chicken thighs
  • 2 big things of broccoli
  • 1.5 oz sesame seeds
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 4ish bulbs of garlic
  • some ginger idk
  • 1 tsp butter
  1. chop chicken
  2. melt butter in pan
  3. brown chicken with garlic and ginger
  4. blanch broccoli in boiling pot for about 2 mins
  5. strain broccoli and put it in the pan with chicken
  6. pour sesame oil and soy sauce mix over chicken and broccoli with sesame seeds
  7. cook until chicken done
